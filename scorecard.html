③ Two locks (the safety net)

The most important design choice

Because my scrambler is home-made and not yet certified by outside experts, I never let it be the only thing guarding your data. Your data always sits behind AES too — the exact lock banks and governments use. Two independent locks, one inside the other.

Your valuables go in a safe, and that safe goes inside a bank vault. A thief has to break both — and the inner one is world-class.

Technical
  • Outer wallmy chaos cipher (sacrificial)
  • Inner vaultAES-256-GCM
  • Vault optionChaCha20-Poly1305
  • Key splitHKDF-SHA256 (never shared)
